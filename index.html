Run with us

The Dasani Marathon is the pinnacle of achievement for everyone who aspires to have healthy and active lifestyle.Whether your dream is to break the world record someday or just get to the finish line, The Dasani Marathon offers something to everyone.

The Race follows a fast and flat route through several beautiful areas of Dar es Salaam and offers glimpses of the turquoise Indian Ocean to help keep you pushing to the finish line.With regular water stops,roads closures , effective marshalling and medical assistance the race promises to both as safe and secure asit is thrilling.Come and join us and make this Dasani Marathon one to remember

On Course Support

Amenities

5 aid stations are located along the course approximately 3 to 4 km apart. The first one from the 5Km each aid station consists of the following amenities in this order:

  • Medical Tent
  • Toilet facilities
  • Water

Medical support

Medical support will be available at 6 on-course locations (5 aid stations and an additional medical tent in the final mile). Medical tents can be identified with the sign medical. Each tent is equipped with a team of certified medical personnel, ambulances, emergency and first aid supplies.
